My name is Stephen Samuel Oluwatobi, the creative director, Samstev Lensman, a professional photographer deeply committed to capturing the beauty of life’s fleeting moments and preserving them as timeless works of art. With a portfolio boasting over 20,000 remarkable images, I’ve been privileged to document a wide range of occasions, from high-profile conferences and electrifying concerts to soul-stirring theatrical performances, impactful village medical outreaches, and treasured milestones lke induction ceremonies and convocation celebrations. For me, photography is much more than a profession—it is a profound way of connecting with people and bringing their unique stories to life. Each photograph I take is an opportunity to preserve emotions, highlight intricate details, and create lasting memories that can be revisited and cherished for generations.
