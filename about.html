About Me

I am Stephen Samuel Oluwatobi, the creative director, Samstev Lensman, a professional photographer deeply committed to capturing the beauty of life’s fleeting moments and preserving them as timeless works of art. With a portfolio boasting over 20,000 remarkable images, I’ve been privileged to document a wide range of occasions, from high-profile conferences and electrifying concerts to soul-stirring theatrical performances, impactful village medical outreaches, and treasured milestones lke induction ceremonies and convocation celebrations. For me, photography is much more than a profession—it is a profound way of connecting with people and bringing their unique stories to life. Each photograph I take is an opportunity to preserve emotions, highlight intricate details, and create lasting memories that can be revisited and cherished for generations.

At the heart of my craft is an unwavering passion for storytelling. I believe that every moment has a narrative worth telling, whether it’s he excitement of a bustling event, the quiet joy of a personal achievement, or the shared laughter of a community gathering. Through my lens, I strive to immortalize these experiences, ensuring that each image carries the essence of the moment it represents. Beyond the technical aspects of photography, I find true fulfillment in the connections I build with my clients and subjects. It is my mission to make people feel seen, celebrated, and understood through the art I create. Photography is not just abou capturing images; it is about crafting visual legacies that honor the beauty, emotions, and memories of life’s journey.

Beyond technical skills, I have an exceptional ability to connect with people. Whether photographing clients or collaborating with a team, I make everyone feel at ease, creating an environment that brings out the best in everyone involved. This emotional intelligence is reflected in my work, as every photograph seems to resonate with authenticity and warmth. My dedication to the craft is inspiring. I'm not just a photographer—I'm an artist who constantly seeks to innovate and improve. I'm always learning experimenting, and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible, which makes their work stand out in any setting.
